  • Why use CampusConnect?

    We're glad you asked. After talking to students, many informed us that current avenues of finding internships aren't user friendly, fail to properly screen opportunities, and prevent students from building relationships with companies. We hope to do just that.

    Attempting to balance academics, extracurricular activities, and sleep is hard, especially as a student at Swarthmore. On top of everything you already have to do, you're expected to find internships, which are now an essential part of finding a job after college. It's hard. Trust us, we get it - that's why we created CampusConnect.

    No one has time to spend pouring over the 7th page of a Google search, desperately searching for a meaningful internship opportunity. But don't worry, we're here to help. Additionally, we will provide each user with a mentor to guide them through every step of the application process, from reviewing resumes to conducting practice interviews.


CampusConnect is a great way to get a head start in the real world.

Our accurate, comprehensive, and user-friendly search engine matches students with job and internship opportunities based on a series of user-generated inputs, such as desired location and pay. Our detailed screening processes ensure that opportunities listed on CampusConnect are legitimate and provide value.
